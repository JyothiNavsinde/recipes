Chicken

Chicken curry is a flavorful dish made by simmering tender chicken pieces in a rich, aromatic sauce. With spices like turmeric, cumin, and coriander, it offers a delightful balance of warmth and depth of flavor. Often served with rice or bread, chicken curry is a hearty and satisfying meal enjoyed in various culinary traditions worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (bone-in or boneless)
  • Cooking oil or ghee
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Ginger, grated or minced
  • Tomatoes, diced or tomato puree
  • Spices: such as turmeric, cumin, coriander, chili powder, and garam masala
  • Salt, to taste
  • Water or chicken broth
  • Optional: Yogurt or coconut milk for creaminess
  • Optional: Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat cooking oil or ghee in a large skillet or pot over medium heat.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown
  3. Stir in minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Add diced tomatoes or tomato puree to the skillet and cook until they break down and form a thick sauce.
  5. Add the chicken pieces to the skillet and season with turmeric, cumin, coriander, chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Stir well to coat the chicken evenly with the spices.
  6. Pour in water or chicken broth until the chicken is just covered.
  7. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it cook for about 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  8. If using, stir in yogurt or coconut milk for creaminess, and adjust seasoning according to taste.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves before serving.
  10. Serve hot with rice or bread.

Enjoy your homemade chicken curry! Adjust the spice levels and ingredients according to your preferences.
