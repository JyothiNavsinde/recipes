Maggie

Maggie noodles, a quick and beloved comfort meal, are made by boiling soft noodles in water and seasoning them with Maggie masala for a burst of flavor. Easily customizable with added vegetables, they're a go-to choice for a simple, satisfying dish enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Maggie noodles(1 packet)
  • Water(around 2 cups)
  • Maggie masala (included in the packet)
  • Optional: Chopped vegetables like carrots, peas, and bell peppers for added flavor and nutrition.

Steps

  1. Boil water in a saucepan.
  2. Add Maggie noodles to the boiling water and cook for 2-3 minutes until they soften.
  3. Stir in the Maggie masala seasoning packet.
  4. Optional: Add chopped vegetables if desired and cook for another 1-2 minutes until tender.
  5. Turn off the heat and let the noodles sit for a minute before serving.

    6. That's it! Your Maggie noodles are ready to enjoy.
